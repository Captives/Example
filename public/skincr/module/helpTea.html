Tips for Common Technical Problems

Students can’t hear me?
Close all the browsers and open the classroom again.
Students can’t see me? Or I can’t see the student?
Try to reaccess the classroom.
I can’t hear the student?
Check if your headset plug well, the switch on the headset is open, or whether your system be muted. Plugin your headset and restart the browsers, access the classroom .
